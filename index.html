About Williams Racing

A Legendary Heritage

Williams Racing is one of the most successful teams in Formula 1 history. Founded by Sir Frank Williams and Patrick Head in 1977, the team has claimed 9 Constructors' Championships and 7 Drivers' Championships.

Based in Grove, Oxfordshire, Williams has been at the forefront of innovation in motorsport for over four decades. The team continues to push boundaries in engineering excellence while honoring its rich heritage.

Team Principal

James Vowles

Team Principal & CEO
"Williams has an incredible heritage, and my goal is to restore this team to its rightful place at the front of the grid. We have the talent, the facilities, and the determination to make it happen."

James Vowles joined Williams Racing as Team Principal in February 2023, bringing over 20 years of experience in Formula 1. Previously serving as Chief Strategist at Mercedes-AMG Petronas, James was instrumental in their unprecedented success, contributing to eight consecutive Constructors' Championships.

Under his leadership, Williams is undergoing a comprehensive transformation, focusing on infrastructure upgrades, talent acquisition, and a renewed commitment to engineering excellence.
